+## Overview

+- Highlights: Om Beach, Kudle Beach, Half Moon Beach, Paradise Beach, Mahabaleshwar Temple
+- Travel: Accessible via Goa (Dabolim) or Mangalore airports; nearest railway station is Gokarna Road
